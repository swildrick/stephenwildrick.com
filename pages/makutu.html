Makutu's Island Logo

Located in Chandler, Arizona, Makutu's Island is a unique indoor play center and family fun destination, designed to immerse children in a tropical island adventure. While collaborating with the owner, I played a key role in shaping the brand by leading the art direction, gathering essential information to craft compelling marketing materials, and developing the website to enhance the center's online presence. Through strategic design and branding, I helped bring the island's vibrant and adventurous spirit to life.
