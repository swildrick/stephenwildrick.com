Dr. Suess Express Yourself! Sweepstakes


As a Senior Interactive Designer, I contributed to the creation of an engaging and intuitive microsite for the Express Yourself! Sweepstakes, celebrating the release of Dr. Seuss's Horse Museum. The sweepstakes encouraged young artists to submit their unique artistic interpretations of The Cat in the Hat using any medium, with parents and caregivers uploading their children's artwork for a chance to win exciting prizes.

  • User-Friendly Submission Process - A simple, guided form allowing parents to easily photograph and upload their child's artwork.
  • Responsive Design - Ensuring the microsite functioned smoothly across all devices, from desktops to mobile phones.
  • Interactive Elements - Engaging animations and dynamic feedback to enhance user engagement.
  • Performance Optimization - Implementing image compression, lazy loading, and optimized JavaScript execution to improve speed and efficiency.

The microsite not only provided a visually appealing and accessible platform but also helped Dr. Seuss Enterprises reconnect with parents and young readers, making the sweepstakes a fun and memorable experience.
