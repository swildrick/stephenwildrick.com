Dr. Suess Express Yourself! Sweepstakes


To mark the 25th anniversary of Oh, the Places You'll Go!, Dr. Seuss Enterprises awarded academic scholarships to outstanding young students, aiming to reconnect with parents who cherished the book in their youth.

The Express Yourself! sweepstakes was launched to celebrate the release of Dr. Seuss's Horse Museum. This contest invited participants to showcase their unique artistic interpretation of The Cat in the Hat.

Parents and caregivers could submit their children's creative renditions using any artistic medium. To enter, they simply had to photograph the artwork and upload it to the Express Yourself! Sweepstakes microsite for a chance to win.
